About Us

We’re young! Incorporated in 2020, we aim at providing best quality home furnishing products at most competitive price. We’re located at Greater NOIDA (adjacent to National Capital, New Delhi), India and operate globally.

We’re a leading Wholesaler, Trader and Merchant Exporter of an extensive array of Bed sheets, Cushion Covers, Towels and other Home Furnishing items.

Our offered range consists of best grade Bedsheet Fabric, Nursery Bedsheet, and 3D Flower Bedsheet. These products are made using high-quality inputs at the vendors’ unit. Our products are appreciated for their easy to use, strong stitching, resistance to shrink, high softness, and lightweight features.

We have appointed hardworking professionals who with their painstaking efforts have helped us to remain at par with our competitors in terms quality of our wide range. Our sourcing agents survey the market for trusted vendors, who are famous for providing quality products. Apart from this, we have experienced quality controllers who keep a close watch on the sourced products in order to deliver defect free range to clients. Also, these products range can be offered in different colors choices as per the specific demands of the customers.

We customize products(hyperlink to Products tab) as per our client needs in minimum amount of time and at best competitive rates.
